## What is MyVacation?

Enjoy the untouched beaches, mountains, lakes, and many more pleasing destinations as well as the magnificent city skylines throughout the country. And when you decide to see them all, a visit won’t be enough to embrace the wonders of Indonesia.

![MyVacation Dashboard](/demo.png)

## MyVacation can Help You:

- Find the best destinations
- Empower you to share your destinations knowledge and opinions with the world’s largest community of traveller

## Platform

MyVacation is available across a range of devices, including computers, phones, tablets,
and you can easily transition from one to another.

- See what destinations your friends are visiting.
- Track the destinations you're have visit, and want to visit.
- Check out your personalized destination recommendations.
- Find out if a destination is a good fit for you from our community’s reviews.

<Callout>You can also browse through the collections of destinations, videos, and inspirations.</Callout>

## Our Mission

Our mission is to help people find and share destinations they love.
